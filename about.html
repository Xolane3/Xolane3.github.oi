My History

My name is Xolane Shabalala. I hail from the serene town of Dannhauser in KwaZulu-Natal, where I began my educational journey at Mana High School. My interest in accounting led me to study the subject from 2012 to 2018, building a solid academic foundation. In 2019, I enrolled at Tshwane University of Technology, pursuing a National Diploma in Information Technology: Multimedia. This period was transformative, allowing me to blend technology with creativity through web design, graphic design, and video production. I completed my theoretical studies in 2023. To stay updated with technological advancements, I completed an 8-week online course titled "Artificial Intelligence Career Essentials" in 2024. Currently, I am seeking Work Integrated Learning (WIL) opportunities to gain practical experience and apply my knowledge in real-world settings. Driven by curiosity and a commitment to continuous learning, I am eager to contribute my diverse skill set to innovative projects. I value community, creativity, and collaboration and look forward to connecting with like-minded individuals and organizations to create impactful solutions.
